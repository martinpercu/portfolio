About Me
Hi, I’m Martin. Born in Argentina, I moved to Paris at 20 to study classical music as a percussionist. My passion for tech started in 2008 when I launched my first business, designing my own website and e-commerce platform.

In 2017, I founded a startup in the beverage industry, leading the tech team. Unfortunately, the pandemic forced us to close, but I’ve since been freelancing as a software developer, helping others bring their ideas to life.

When I’m not working, you can find me surfing whenever I get the chance.
